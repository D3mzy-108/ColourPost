How it Works

ColourPost simplifies your paint manufacturing operations by organizing raw materials, production, and sales in a seamless flow. Here's how to quickly get started to maximize the platform's potential:

1. Sign In

  • Go to the ColourPost login page.
  • Enter your registered username and password.
  • Click Sign In to access your dashboard.
2. Set Up Raw Materials

  • Navigate to "Inventory"
  • Open the "Raw Materials" tab and click on "Add New Material" button.
  • Fill in the required details (e.g Material Name, Quantity, and Unit Cost), and save the entry
  • Repeat for all raw materials.
3. Create Product Categories

  • Navigate to "Production" and click the "Product Categories" button, then click on "Create Category".
  • Provide the Category Name and the Raw Materials required to produce it, then save the entry.
  • Add additional categories as needed to organize your products.
4. Set Up Packaging Options

From "Production", Navigate to "Packaging" tab, then click on "Add Packaging". Next, define packaging sizing and alias, and save the entry.

Creating a production batch in ColourPost is a straightforward process. First, navigate to the "Production" tab and click "Create New Batch." You'll then enter the batch details, including a name (e.g., Batch001), the product category (e.g., Silk Paint), and select the raw materials used, specifying the quantity of each. ColourPost automatically calculates the total batch cost based on these raw material inputs. After saving the batch, you can view the finished products by navigating to the "Inventory" tab and selecting the "Finished Product" section. Here, you can confirm the quantities produced, and these finished products will automatically be reflected in your inventory.
